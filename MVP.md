1. Executive Summary

Mindchain is a digital infrastructure designed to enable automated payments between machines (M2M) and between artificial intelligences (AI-AI), without direct human intervention, using a system of programmable service credits.

The Mindchain MVP aims to:

demonstrate technical feasibility

validate real, non-speculative demand

support early industrial and AI-driven use cases

Mindchain is not a speculative cryptocurrency, but a service-oriented payment and settlement layer for autonomous systems.

2. Problem Statement

Today:

3. The Mindchain Solution (MVP Scope)

Mindchain introduces:

Machine-based wallets

Programmable service credits

Lightweight, off-chain-first transactions

Periodic settlement

Auditable transaction logs

The MVP focuses on infrastructure validation, not token speculation.
